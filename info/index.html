About

Designer/illustrator in Vancouver, BC.

I work with print and digital projects, doing illustration, product, visual and interaction design. Besides design I have an academic background in psychology and used to work in behavioral research, which helped me develop an analytical, behavioral-based perspective to solving design challenges.

Since then I’ve worked in advertising and branding agencies and currently am an independent designer.

Get in touch with me via contact@deadtype.ca.
