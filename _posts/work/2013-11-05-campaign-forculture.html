“Campaign for Culture” is a grassroots organization looking to modernize liquor laws in British Columbia. Their work involves government officials as well as the general public. To help them reach these groups I developed a professional, yet visually striking and accessible design language.

{% include system/divider_label_param.html param='Branding' %}

The design of the identity combined clear, readable typography with simple, colorful illustrations:

{% include system/figure_centered.html background='see_through ' background_color='gray' outer_width='feed_wrap' inner_width='content_wrap' figure='logo.png' %}

Logo is a set of radiating rings — representing 5 individual phases which build into the full campaign. Together with the wordmark it creates a flexible combination that works in a variety of print and digital contexts.

{% include system/figure_centered.html background='see_through ' background_color='gray' outer_width='content_media_center' inner_width='feed_wrap' figure='warnock.png' %}

Warnock Pro is used to set the wordmark and all of the campaign’s print and online content. Its classic look is appropriate for official documents, while the sharp design of its serifs gives text a subtle quirkiness and a modern feel.

The illustrations are used in advertising, promotional materials and social media. They are drawn modular, ensuring that individual pieces are easy to take apart, art-direct and customize with headlines or copy.

{% include system/divider_label_param.html param='Website' %}

Campaign for Culture website combines typography and illustrations into a stylish presentation. It is built on a simple one-column grid with a focus on readability. By adjusting text size, leading and measure with changing screen sizes a tailored experience is created for a range of devices.

{% include system/figure_centered.html inner_width='content_media_secondary bracket' background_color='' outer_width='' figure='web_campaign.jpg' %}

The site details specific issues the campaign focused on and provides a way for voters to connect with government officials regarding these topics.

{% include system/figure_centered.html inner_width='content_media_secondary bracket' background_color='' outer_width='' figure='web_form.jpg' %}

Campaign for Culture also hosts a feed of news and articles related to the project and the issues it is addressing.
